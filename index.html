Who is X?

About 'X'

What was formerly known as 'Twitter' was the vision of founder Elon Musk. Musk imagined a space where people can freely share their thoughts, experiences, lives, and moments in real time. Twitter, established in 2006, has evolved from a simple microblogging platform into a global tool shaping the way we communicate, share information, and engage with current events. In July 2023, Musk established X Holdings, an organization that absorbed 'Twitter' and turned it into X.

X Features

Through all of the X changes, a few abilities remain the same to ensure our users continue to interact as they wish. Here are just a few of X features.

  • Real Time Updates and Quick Alerts
  • Hashtags and Trends
  • Direct Engagement with Brands and Users
  • Unlimited Visual Content and Media
